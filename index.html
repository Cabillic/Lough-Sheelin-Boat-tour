Lough Sheelin

Lough Sheelin (from Irish Loch Síodh Linn 'lake of the fairy pool'), in standard Irish Loch Síleann, is a limestone freshwater lough (lake) in central Ireland. The lake is a part of the River Inny course, and ultimately of the Shannon system.

It lies at a meeting point of Counties Westmeath, Meath and Cavan, near the villages of Finnea and Mountnugent, and the town of Granard, in County Longford. More than half its area is in County Cavan, and it takes in parts of multiple civil parishes and baronies.
